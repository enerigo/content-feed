BatBus (“the app”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data may be collected when you use BatBus and why it is used. BatBus is published in Google Play and may be used in Georgia and other countries, including the European Economic Area (EEA).

What data we collect ↑

BatBus does not ask you to create an account and we do not collect information such as your name, email, or phone number.

  • App usage & diagnostics
    Basic events like app screens visited, interactions, and crash reports may be collected through Firebase services.
  • Anonymous identifiers
    The app may use an anonymous identifier (e.g., Firebase installation/app instance identifier) to support features like news read status and VIP entitlements.
  • Advertising identifiers (if ads are enabled)
    Google AdMob may use device advertising identifiers and related signals to serve ads, including rewarded ads. See Google’s policy for details.

How we use data

App improvement

Monitor stability, investigate crashes, and improve performance.

Remote configuration

Enable/disable features (ads, VIP rewards, routes, news) without forcing app updates.

Advertising & rewards

Show ads and rewarded ads and grant VIP time when applicable.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

BatBus may display ads using Google AdMob, including rewarded ads. AdMob may collect and process data such as advertising identifiers and device information to serve and measure ads.

You can control ad personalization in your Android settings (see Your choices).

Firebase services

BatBus may use the following Google Firebase products:

  • Firebase Analytics
    Helps us understand general app usage (aggregated).
  • Firebase Crashlytics
    Helps us detect crashes and fix issues faster.
  • Firebase Remote Config
    Controls feature flags and app parameters (ads/news/routes/VIP settings).
  • Firebase Firestore
    May store news content and anonymous entitlement data (VIP/no-ads expiration).

Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

VIP / Rewards

BatBus can grant temporary VIP features (for example, after a rewarded ad or via promo/coupon logic if enabled). VIP status is represented by an expiration timestamp stored locally on your device and/or in Firebase Firestore using an anonymous identifier.

VIP data may include: - vipUntil (timestamp) - noAdsUntil (timestamp) - readNewsIds (list of IDs) (No name/email/phone is stored.)

News content

BatBus may display in-app news published by the app owner. News entries may be stored in Firebase Firestore and loaded by the app. Your device may store which news items you have read to show an “unread” indicator.

EEA / GDPR notice (for EU/EEA users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, we process data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests
    App stability, security, debugging, and basic analytics to improve the service.
  • Consent (where required)
    For personalized ads, consent may be requested via Google’s consent mechanism (e.g., UMP) depending on region and settings.

Note: If you enable/serve ads in the EEA, it’s recommended to implement Google UMP consent flow before initializing ads.

Your choices & controls

  • Reset app data
    You can clear the app’s data from Android settings or reinstall the app.
  • Ad personalization
    Android Settings → Google → Ads (or Privacy) lets you control ad personalization and advertising identifier usage.
  • Opt-out choices
    Some features may reduce or disable ads (e.g., VIP/no-ads time) depending on remote configuration.

Children

BatBus is not intended for children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child has used the app in a way that concerns you, please contact us.
